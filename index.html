About L4HK

L4HK is a collaborative project to crowdsource and document Hong Kong's journey to achieving meaningful freedom, democracy, and autonomy. This project was first created in the wake of the 2019 Anti-Extradition Bill protests.

Liberate Hong Kong, A Revolution of Our Time

The 2019 protests were more than merely opposition against a legislative bill. It was a fully fledged movement that helped Hong Kongers discovered their identity, and generated large volumes of cultural artefacts such as music and art.
